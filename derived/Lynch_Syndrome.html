review_notes ?
Lynch syndrome is caused by inherited mutations in mismatch repair genes, leading to a very high lifetime risk of colorectal and endometrial cancers. Risks of other cancers like stomach, ovary, and urinary tract are also elevated. Screening and prophylactic surgery can reduce cancer incidence and mortality.
